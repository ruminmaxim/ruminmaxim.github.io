MacBattery

About

Utility to work with battery and check power usage information for Apple laptops. The application could be used and purchased as a standalone product. But its main purpose was to be an auxiliary application for promoting the sales of another program of the company (which was the main product). The idea was to sell programs as a bundle at a discount.
