About projects and results

The site was launched back in 2016, however, a well-chosen neutral UI («timeless»), competent UX, well-prepared bitmaps and the latest mechanics at that time allowed the site to work for almost 6 years without major changes, while the content of the site was updated and the site «lived». This is a very long lifespan of website design, especially for such a dynamic and competitive environment as web studio services. During this time, the site converted a very large number of leads, received positive reviews from customers (the design was «catchy»). In 2022, the concept of the studio was revised and the site was replaced with a new one.
